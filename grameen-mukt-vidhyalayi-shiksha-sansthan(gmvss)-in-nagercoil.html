The Department of Open Schooling at UNIVERSITY DISTANCE AND ONLINE EDUCATION CENTER | ONLINE DEGREE COLLEGE - THUCKALAY | NAGERCOIL | GUMIDIPOONDI offers a flexible and alternative approach to education, allowing students to learn outside the traditional classroom setting. This program is tailored to support individuals who face barriers to attending regular school, including geographical distance, physical disabilities, family obligations, or work commitments.

Open schooling programs are designed to serve a diverse range of learners, including: Students who are unable to attend regular school due to geographical isolation or mobility constraints.

Individuals who have discontinued formal education and wish to resume their studies, Working professionals looking to enhance their skills or pursue further education while balancing work and family responsibilities, Open schooling is an inclusive approach to education that empowers individuals to achieve academic success and personal growth, regardless of their circumstances. It promotes lifelong learning and skills development, advancing individuals and communities globally.

Open and Distance Learning (ODL) is the provision of distance education opportunities in ways that seek to mitigate or remove barriers to access, such as finances, prior learning, age, social, work or family commitments, disability , incarceration or other such barriers. “Open” refers to a commitment that removes any unnecessary barriers to access learning. Distance education refers to teaching and learning that temporarily separates teacher and learner in time and/or place; uses multiple media for delivery of instruction; involves two-way communication and possibly occasional face-to-face meeting for tutorials and learner-learner interaction. Open learning is not the same as distance learning, but both are complementary and hence the two terms are often used together as open and distance learning.
