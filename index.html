How to Apply?

  • Students can download the admission form from the website or collect directly from the Institute with the prescribed fee. For all the courses the same admission form is applicable.
  • Submit the duly filled application form to the admission department of the Institute with the following documents along with the course fee.
  • Educational Certificate Starting From Class 10th, 12th, ITI, Diploma/ B.Sc All Mark sheet and T.C Soft Copy Or Hard Copy.
  • ID proof, Address proof with Parents name should be in English.
  • Affidavit ( download from the download section of Institute Website ).
  • Migration/Transfer certificate from previous board/university.
  • Working company name, Number of year experience, Passport size photos (5), Contact no.
  • All the above should be in a clear and neat scan documents mail us to:gokulameducationalinstituions@gmail.com or send the hard copy of documents directly to our institute.
